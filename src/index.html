Timers

A unique kind of clock known as a timer is used to record certain periods of time.Two categories can be used to classify timers. The term "timer" is typically used to describe devices that begin counting down from a predetermined time, while stopwatches are gadgets that perform the reverse, measuring the amount of time that has passed by counting up from zero. Scroll and read below to see the timers used by CPCS.

Countdown

A Countdown timer is a clock that counts down from a certain number or date to indicate the beginning or end of an event that is scheduled to occur.

Stopwatch

A Stopwatch timer is a clock created with the intention of measuring the time that elapses between its activation and deactivation.
